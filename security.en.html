Full Home Security

Complete peace of mind, wherever you are

Smart home security systems possess several significant advantages over traditional home security models. With expanded and more effective capabilities, homeowners are increasingly inquiring about these to better protect themselves and loved ones from outside threats.

More than just alarms, an integrated security system can use lights for occupancy simulation, combine a variety of sensors and CCTV, and alert you of any danger, wherever you are.

Creating the Perfect Atmosphere at the Touch of a Button or Whispering a Voice Command

Fabulous lighting can make a room, create a mood, or just put a smile on your face. Alter the mood of a room to suit the occasion at the touch of button. Set lighting to automatically adjust to daylight conditions or simulate occupancy even when on holiday.

Reduction in crime is of great concern to everyone. Smart lighting enables lights to behave as though there was someone at home. With smart lighting users can change colour or brightness in every room and even control them from a smartphone from anywhere in the world.

Sensors & Alarms

Be More Informed About Your Home than Ever Before.

A new breed of unobtrusive, wireless sensors provides accurate, real-time information, giving us a more holistic view of our home and environment...

Live data can tell us where energy is being used and by continuously tracking and collecting this data, you can identify energy wastage and savings. Sensors can also be used to manage and maintain assets, with monitors able to detect any problems and switch off any assets when not in use. Access Control

It's Time to Upgrade Your Deadbolt

The entire idea of a smart lock (and every smart-home device, really) is to add convenience to your life. For smart locks, that includes features like remote access, voice control and keyless entry.

Without the need to make key copies or have a spare, you'll have fewer methods of entry to your home floating around in the form of physical keys. You can even set some smart locks to automatically lock the door after a set amount of time, or when your cell phone has left the immediate area.

No more making key copies or hiding a spare set. Instead, you can give out digital keys using the lock's accompanying app and even restrict what times someone is allowed to enter your home.
