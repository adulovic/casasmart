About Us

About Us

Traditional family values meet futuristic technology

Since the beginning of time, rich people delegated time consuming tasks to their employees or slaves, thus providing themselves with more of the ultimate commodity: time. The increasingly lower prices of automation products and voice assistants simply mean that now everyone can afford delegating tasks to IoT (Internet of Things) devices instead, and I can tell you from experience that they are not only cheaper, but also more reliable.

As anyone who owns a house with a garden will tell you, the work around a house is never finished! Since purchasing our first house in 2010, we have been quite involved in improving it over the weekends and even holidays. This gradually led to realizing which tasks can be automated, and that remote controlling lights and appliances are an additional bonus. It is so much more convenient to just ask your house for information or issue a command that is instantly executed, and avoid having to walk out in a thunderstorm just to make sure the pool pump is switched off... (been there, done that). Before we knew it, we reached a point of having over 100 smart devices in and around the house. Everything is automated, and the kids say they feel like living with the Jetsons or Tony Stark.

To cut a long story short, our friends and neighbors kept asking us for help with smart home automation, and after years of listening to their suggestions that we offer professional services to the wider community, we finally registered a company and created this webpage in 2020.

Explore the Possibilities

When it comes time to sell a smart home, sellers will have an abundance of effective selling points. Whichever advantage of a smart home appeals to a given buyer, the seller can explain the system and discuss how it makes life easier. Homes with automated systems have the potential to sell for much more than comparable homes with conventional technologies. Automating a home can be a worthwhile investment in increasing its market value and attracting possible buyers in the future.
