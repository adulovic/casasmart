Services

Professional Services

We design and install simple to use solutions that enhance our clients homes, businesses and lifestyles

Our solutions are modular, meaning you can have as much or as little Smart Home functionality as you want now, or add to it later when time or budget allows. Every system is tailored to your requirements and as unique as you are.

Besides helping you keep your costs low, a smart home can typically save you from doing around 50,000 tasks per year. Apart from the increased comfort and peace of mind, just imagine all the extra time this can afford you.

We don`t stop work when your system is installed. Technology changes daily so when you need to add a function to your system, we are ready to help. We can also help fix or finish installations begun by others.

Consulting

Our Experts Will Save You Time and Money

Our affordable consulting services will help guide you through personalized custom solutions to save you research time and have your smart home designed exactly to your preferences.

Recommendations will be made for products and services that will fit in with your desires and budget. We'll even provide you with an inventory list, so you can review the products before purchasing from us or purchase them yourself, directly from other reliable retailers such as Amazon.

There are two schools of thought on the installation of smart home products: DIY and DIFM. Do It Yourself (DIY) is a lot about cost savings. I can buy a smart speaker or a Nest thermostat and install it myself, at a time that’s convenient for me, without paying extra for it. Do It For Me (DIFM) is what was previously known as ‘professional installation’: I will contract someone not only to install my smart home products but possibly also to recommend which products to buy, design how they would be installed in my home, and potentially monitor and support these products after the installation.
